## Introduction

Masscer ("we", "us") operates the Masscer platform at [app.masscer.ai](https://app.masscer.ai) and related services. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. Contact: [masscer.ai@gmail.com](mailto:masscer.ai@gmail.com).

**This document is a product template and should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before production use.**

## Information we collect

- **Account data:** name, email address, password (stored hashed), organization membership, and profile preferences you provide.
- **Usage data:** conversations, messages, agent configurations, workflows, and metadata needed to operate the service.
- **Files and attachments** you upload or import (including content pulled from connected integrations).
- **Billing and subscription** information processed by our payment providers.
- **Technical data:** IP address, browser type, device information, and logs used for security and reliability.

## Google account data

If you connect Google services or sign in with Google:

- We receive **basic profile information** (such as name and email) via Google OAuth scopes `openid`, `email`, and `profile`.
- If you connect **Google Calendar**, we access calendar data according to the `calendar` scope to list, create, and update events on your behalf when you use Masscer features or agents. We do not sell this data.
- If you connect **Google Drive**, we access files according to the `drive.readonly` scope when you use Drive-related features.

Google OAuth tokens are stored securely on our servers and used only to perform actions you request. You can disconnect integrations in **Integrations** settings or revoke access in your [Google Account permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions).

We use Google APIs in compliance with the [Google API Services User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy), including Limited Use requirements.

## Other third-party services

We use subprocessors to run the product (for example cloud hosting, email delivery, and AI model providers). They process data only to provide the service on our instructions. Model providers may process message content to generate responses when you use AI features.

## How we use information

We use data to provide and improve Masscer, authenticate users, enforce organization policies, billing, support, security, and legal compliance.

## Retention

Organization administrators may configure retention for deleted conversations and attachments. Other data is retained while your account is active and as needed for legal or operational purposes.
